If you’re paying ~$1000/month for a receptionist and still missing 8–10 daily calls worth ~$50 each, AI handles all calls 24/7 → you remove salary cost and recover ~$8000–$12000 monthly lost revenue

If 100 people contact you but only 40 book due to delays, AI responds instantly and pushes that to 65–70 bookings → same traffic, but ~30 more paying customers without hiring anyone.

If you’re spending 4 hours daily answering 200 messages where 150 are repetitive, AI handles most of them → you save ~120 hours/month and avoid hiring a $500–$800 support staff

If 100 customers message you and only 10–15 buy because of slow replies, instant AI responses and follow-ups convert 20–30 → same traffic, but double revenue from existing inquiries
